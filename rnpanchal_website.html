About Us

Your Trusted Caster Wheel Partner


R N Panchal Fab & Engineers was founded in the year 2000 with a single vision: to deliver industrial caster wheels of uncompromising quality at factory-direct prices. Based in Ahmedabad — India's industrial heartland — we have spent over two and a half decades serving factories, warehouses, hospitals, logistics hubs, and material handling industries across India.

We manufacture and supply the full range of caster wheels — from light-duty office and furniture applications to extreme heavy-duty industrial uses. Our manufacturing process follows strict quality checks at every stage, ensuring each wheel that leaves our facility meets the highest standards of load capacity, durability, and smooth rolling performance.
